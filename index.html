About Us

Resolving Legal Challenges with Commitment and Care

Pratikar Law Firm is your most trusted and comprehensive legal service provider based in Guwahati, Assam, serving the entire Northeast India region. Established in 2013 with a deep commitment to justice, we have been consistently delivering expert, reliable legal services to clients across Assam, Nagaland, Meghalaya, Mizoram, and beyond.

At Pratikar Law Firm, we believe in delivering tailored legal services with a strong focus on our clients' individual needs. With extensive experience across multiple legal domains, our team is equipped to provide clear, effective representation and advice. From intricate legal disputes to general counsel, we stand by your side throughout the legal journey.

Our strong grasp of both local and national legal frameworks enables us to pursue the best results for you. We value the trust you place in us—and we remain fully committed to your success.
